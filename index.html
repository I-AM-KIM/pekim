About Pekimo Bakery

Pekimo Bakery is a proudly Kenyan bakery located in the heart of Kutus, Kirinyaga County. We specialize in freshly baked bread, cakes, pastries, and custom orders for all occasions. Our goal is to bring joy to every table with our delicious and affordable baked goods.
